FEATURE OVERVIEW
================================================================================

WHAT IS IT?
A gamification layer that rewards users for marketplace activity and 
redemptions, creating incentives for ecosystem engagement and improving 
platform utility.

HOW IT WORKS:
- Users earn 1 point per STX redeemed
- Users earn 1 point per STX spent on marketplace
- Users earn 0.5 points per STX earned from sales
- 10 points = 1% marketplace fee reduction
- Maximum discount capped at 50%
- Discounts applied automatically

EXAMPLE:
User with 100 loyalty points buys gift card for 1000 STX
Standard fee: 2.5% = 25 STX
Loyalty discount: 10% (100 points / 10)
Discounted fee: 22.5 STX
User saves: 2.5 STX
