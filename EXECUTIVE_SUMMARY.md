## 🎁 What Users Get

### Earn Loyalty Points
- 1 point per STX redeemed
- 1 point per STX spent on marketplace
- 0.5 points per STX earned from sales

### Redeem for Discounts
- 10 points = 1% marketplace fee reduction
- Up to 50% maximum discount
- Automatic application at checkout

### Example Benefit
```
User with 100 loyalty points
Saves 2.5 STX on a 1000 STX marketplace purchase
(10% discount on 2.5% fee)
```
